Mitsubishi Lancer

How do you prefer to achieve your expectations?

Very diverse, you will fulfil any of your desires and surpass whatever you have been waiting for in a car of its class. Its high-tech advantages alone will impress you, until you discover how easy it is to explore these advantages. If music is what you want, its sound system will captivate your ears. If you prefer performance, Lancer provides you with everything, from economics to great performance. Why do you limit your expectations? With Lancer, you will always be amazed by the way you wish

Abroad

You think she can't resist? Look again

Approach it and scrutinise it, its design will captivate you, but its appeal is more than just a design. All Lancer models carry the spirit of the Evolution model. The solid and unique construction of its design gives it the strength to handle high-performance driving. While it has great flow, it is also practical, with its high wide roof and wide ass. So once you're done staring, you'll find it easier to enter it.

Storage space

The new Lancer offers a large and comfortable rear storage space, providing up to 351 litres of storage capacity. The back cover has been redesigned with side joints to provide more efficient use of the space and avoid it from colliding with your luggage. For added convenience, you can access luggage from inside the cabin through the possibility of folding the back seat at a ratio of 40:60. With the addition of the ground luggage compartment, the trunk area is shortened to a capacity of 315 litres.

Performance

Engine and transmission

However you choose your driving mode, you will inevitably feel the pure pleasure of the rally races inspired by the new Lancer design. It's in the roar of the rising power under the engine, and the wonderful smoothness of the CVT transmission when it moves up or down. Once Lancer speaks to you, you will immediately understand.

1.6-litre engine with MIVEC technology

The 1.6-litre engine gives you a boost of concentrated excitement, where enough power for sports performance has been put wherever you have the opportunity to test it. Just like its great cousin, the engine also provides an aluminum head for improved efficiency, and MIVEC technology for more offensive start and maximum power.

Inside

Enjoy sitting as if you were at home

It's really unfortunate when the design is so wonderful and you can't always look at it. The Lancer dashboard is engineered by placing each control exactly where you expect it to be. Gearshift arms and volume control buttons have also been added to the steering wheel to give you the full control you want without the need to take your gaze off the road ahead.

Mitsubishi Eclipse Cross

Here now, then anywhere

The new Eclipse Cross is Mitsubishi's first SUV designed to impress inside and out. From its sharp, dynamic body lines and sophisticated interior design, to its turbocharged gasoline engine and unparalleled handling, this is a vehicle that impresses every time you get behind the wheel.

outside

Ready for action

Bold lines. Wide fenders. A powerful, deeply sculpted body, just like a muscular man ready to take off from the starting block. Anyone who looks at it will be left contemplating the power of its sloping rear window design.

Inside

Exploration is now under your control

The meticulously designed sports cockpit quickens your pulse and increases your pleasure. Every detail is designed to enhance your awareness and control, bringing intuitive driving through direct and consistent response to your every command.

performance

When life throws you a curveball, stay in control.

Challenges arise when you least expect them. Whether navigating twisty roads or unpaved terrain, the S-AWC system delivers optimal drive torque to the rear wheels, as well as optimal drive and braking torque distribution to the left or right front wheels separately, giving the car an unparalleled sense of stability and control.

Engine

1.5-liter direct-injection turbocharged gasoline engine

The engine has been downsized for maximum efficiency and is turbocharged to add more torque at low to mid-range rpm. This advanced engine can deliver instant acceleration response when you command it.
