Volkswagen is a German automobile manufacturer based in Wolfsburg, Lower Saxony, Germany. Volkswagen was established in 1937 The company is well known for the Beetle and serves as the flagship marque of the Volkswagen Group, which became the world's largest automotive manufacturer by global sales in 2016 and 2017.
